Temple Basin lifts & facilities status

Road :
  • Road Access
    Closed

    The Temple Basin Carpark is located right beside State Highway 73, 4 kms west of Arthurs Pass Village. The road is sealed and well maintained so chains are rarely needed. The free goods lift which carries all gear and supplies up the mountain, is also located beside State Highway 73 but 800m further west from the carpark. The access walking track starts from the west end of the carpark.
