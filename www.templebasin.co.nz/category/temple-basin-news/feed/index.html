Kia Ora and welcome to our Season Pass page. To ski or snowboard as much as you like at Temple Basin this winter you have two choices. Either purchase yourself a Season Pass  or team up with three of your mates and purchase a Buddy Pass. Each member of the Buddy Pass receives their own pass so they can visit the independently of each other. Get in quick as Early Bird prices are valid only until the end of May. For more information on passes please scroll down.

To apply for a Temple Basin Season or Buddy Pass

1: Complete the online application form.
2: Make payment (our office team will process your application and email you an invoice).
3: Send your pass photos to info@templebasin.co.nz

If you require any help or have any queries regarding pass purchases please email info@templebasin.co.nz.

Pass Options

There are two types of season passes available at Temple Basin in 2016.

1. Buddy Passes
2. Season Passes

Buddy Passes are available to four friends purchasing together. The will receive one ‘Buddy Pass’ which entitles each of the four people to the privileges of a season lift pass. Payment of $540 (including 15% GST) must be made at one time and photographs of the four people supplied together.

  • Buddy Passes: Available until 5pm on 31st May 2016

Season Passes entitle the holder to ski or snowboard as much as they like provided the mountain is open. They are available in two price variations (1, Adults. 2, Students/Juniors) and with a discount to those who get in early.

  • Earlybird Season Passes: Available until 5pm on 31st May 2016
  • Season Passes: Available until 5pm on 30 June 2016
